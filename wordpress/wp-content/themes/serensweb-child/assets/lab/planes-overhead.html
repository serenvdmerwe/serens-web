planes overhead

Every aircraft transmitting within 150 nautical miles, on a scope that refreshes while you watch. The pins take a small detour on their way here: browser to this site's own WordPress REST API, which fetches the flight data server-side, caches it for fifteen seconds, and hands back a trimmed list. The aggregators do not allow browsers to call them directly, so WordPress is quietly the radar's backend.

scanning

Starting over False Bay, Cape Town. Tap Center on me to move the scope to your sky; your position goes to this site's endpoint only to pick the search area, and is not stored.

Flight data by the adsb.lol community network of volunteer ADS-B receivers. Coverage depends on volunteer receivers nearby: an empty ocean scope is usually honest, an empty city scope usually means a gap in coverage. Positions are as transmitted by the aircraft.
