Aptitude Trainer

Endless practice for the three puzzle families aptitude tests lean on. Every puzzle is generated on your device the moment you need it: no question bank, no repeats, no account.

Honesty note: this trains familiarity with test patterns. It does not measure intelligence, and nothing here is an IQ score. Progress compares you with your own earlier answers only.
